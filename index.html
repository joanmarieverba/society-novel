About the Author

Deborah K. Vleck (Debby Jones) was a writer, designer, and fiber artist. Creator of many award-winning costumes, she was twice a Best in Show winner of the World Science Fiction Convention Masquerade. She worked as a high school teacher, curriculum researcher, newsletter publisher, database manager, graphic designer, city planner, and full-time mother of two. She was married to a professional astronomer and lived in Canberra, Honolulu, and St. Paul. She was one of the co-authors of the science fiction novel, Autumn World. She passed away on July 8, 2023 and is survived by husband Terry and two adult children, Rhiannon and Bryan.
